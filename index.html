International Coding Hub

About us

The International Coding Hub was founded in April of 2020, during the time of COVID-19. It is a student-led organization dedicated to cultivating interest in programming. We created The International Coding Hub as a place for programmers to exchange ideas, help each other, prepare for coding competitions and technical interviews, and improve.

Our Goal

Our main goal is to cultivate interest in programming, and to promote creative and ingenious thinking. To do this, we have:

  • A weekly problem of the week (PoTW) which ranges from a variety of topics
  • Educational programming competitions with high quality problems
  • Programming classes taught by experienced instructors
  • A constructive space where people can discuss interesting topics, ideas, and problems
  • USACO study groups and help channels
